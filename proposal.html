Project Name: Suzanne Collins’ Personal Website Redesign

Overview: This site features pictures of the covers of her books with descriptions, rankings, reviews, her biography, and an Interview with Scholastic Press. (5 Content areas in total)

Audience:

Fans of Suzanne Collins or anyone who has heard of her works and wants to check them out. Mostly YA (Young Adult); mostly middle schoolers and high schoolers, late elementary for advanced readers. She has also done a few children’s books for younger audiences.
